How to Enter the Global Heritage Pageant

Your journey to the crown begins here

Visit Our Office

Come to our office to submit your application and meet our friendly front desk personnel.

Complete the Form

Fill out the application form with accurate information to kickstart your registration process.

Attend an Interview

Participate in an initial screening interview with our judges to assess your eligibility.

Training and Workshops

Join our expert-led workshops and training sessions to prepare for the big stage.

Steps to Apply

  1. Download or collect the application form.
  2. Provide necessary documents such as your ID and passport photos.
  3. Pay the registration fee.
  4. Submit your application at our office or online.
  5. Prepare for your interview and talent screening.
